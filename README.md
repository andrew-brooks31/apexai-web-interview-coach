## Overview

APEX.AI is designed for students who want interview practice that feels conversational rather than scripted. Rather than relying on a fixed list of prompts, it uses an AI-powered voice interview flow that can respond to answers and keep the session moving with relevant follow-up questions.

The experience supports both answer quality and delivery. In addition to interview-style prompts, the application reviews spoken responses and provides feedback that can help improve technical knowledge, confidence, fluency, clarity, grammar, and general communication.

- Realistic AI interview simulation for practice sessions
- Adaptive follow-up questions based on spoken answers

- Personalized feedback on confidence, fluency, and clarity

## How to Use It

1. Start a practice interview session.
2. Answer the prompts aloud using your microphone or voice input workflow.
3. Review the follow-up questions that adapt to your response.
4. Read the feedback summary to identify areas to improve.
5. Repeat sessions to work on technical answers, fluency, and delivery.

Example workflow:

- Choose an interview topic or practice mode
- Respond to each question naturally
- Pay attention to the next question generated by the coach
- Review feedback after the session
- Apply the notes in your next practice round
